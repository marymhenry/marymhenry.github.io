CopyCat Travel App

CopyCat Travel app was created for travelers that feel overwhelmed in planning their upcoming trip itineraries. With too many resources to look through, travelers don’t know which reviews they can trust. CopyCat Travel app solves this problem by connecting users with the trusted travel reviews of their friends. They can see their friends’ trip itineraries, look at their reviews, connect with their friends, and use their suggestions to help the user decide their travel itinerary.

Roles

UX Researcher and UX Designer

Tools Used

Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator and Miro



USER RESEARCH

Objectives

I want to understand what tools and resources users find most helpful while making travel plans.

I want to understand the degree to which people plan their daily itinerary and activities prior to traveling.

I want to understand what factors or circumstances create the highest amount of stress while traveling.

I want to understand what decisions non-planners make when traveling.

SURVEY RESULTS

In order to expand upon the answers received from our interviews, our team created google forms survey of 8 questions to understand traveling stressors, use and trust of reviews, travel style, as well as frequency of travel. A total of 29 people were surveyed over a period of a few days.

REVIEWS

How often do you rely on reviews when making travel plans?

STRESSORS

Aside from flights and/or transportation, what part of travel planning do you find most stressful?

TRAVEL STYLE

Describe your travel preparation style.

Problem Statement

We believe that creating a trusted and reliable source of information through reviews created by friends and family for travelers that seek exciting, adventurous and unique cultural experiences, will achieve less stress on part of the travelers and higher efficiency when planning their travel itinerary. We will know this is true through reviews of our app, recommendation of our service and feedback from travelers who choose our app first as they begin their travel preparations.

Final Thoughts

General: I have learned so much about the UX/UI process during this, my first, mobile case study app assignment. I really enjoy all aspects of UX - but especially user research and iOS prototype design.

User Research: I have learned to listen to others and take their experiences in order to offer solutions. It’s so important to HEAR what others are saying in order to truly help. Having a better concept of what my group and I wanted to create would have been helpful in the first case, however, I was able to take that information to create an easy to use app to improve their travel experience.

Wireframing: I created my wireframes before I did my user flow, because I am a very visual person. In the future - I will try to do both at the same time.
